Available Financing Options

Our finance and funding options are available for most programs and university partners. We recommend speaking with one of our admissions advisors to review your options in full and find the right financial plan for your studies.

Prepay Option

Choosing to pay in advance for your bootcamp program is the most cost-effective option, as students who pay in full ahead of the start of class receive a discount.

Monthly Installments

Our financing partners, Ascent, EdAid, Meritize, FynnCredit and Sallie Mae, offer a variety of payment plan options in 12, 24, or 36-month installments. Some monthly installment options may require a $500 application fee. Monthly installment plans may vary between our university partners, please speak with one of our admissions advisors to learn more about this option.

Please note: A credit check and approval will be needed to qualify for any payment plan, excluding FynnCredit.
