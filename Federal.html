DoD 8140.01 & DoD 8570.01

Approved Baseline Certifications

Our cybersecurity certification options meet the U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Directive 8140.01 and 8570.01. The updated DoD 8140.01 directive includes additional policies to unify the cybersecurity workforce, define specific responsibilities and set the standards for the various roles.

QuickStart delivers live online, on-demand, and private group trainings to meet the DoD-approved IA baseline certification.
