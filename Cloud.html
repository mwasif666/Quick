Bootcamp Courses

Through our partnerships with top technology organizations, we’re able to offer leading-edge IT training and coursework designed for the future of the field. Each bootcamp course will prepare you with new skills and tools to pass top industry and career-relevant certifications.

Designed for beginners and students without any IT experience, the IT Fundamentals course covers the essentials for a career in information technology. This course is ideal for non-technical professionals and empowers students to use features and functions of common operating systems, establish network connectivity, identify common software applications, and more.

Designed to prepare students for the CompTIA Network+ certification exam (N10-008), this course will focus on the security skills necessary for an entry-level role in cybersecurity.

This course will teach students how to identify security fundamentals and threats; analyze risk; conduct security assessments; implement network, operational, host, and software security; manage identity and access; implement cryptography; address security threats; and ensure business continuity. After completing this course, students will be able to complete CompTIA Sec+ certification.

In this project, students will develop hands-on experience installing and configuring different types of operating systems, networks, networking tools, and security baselines. They will also learn to prepare a project report. *Depending on your personal computer hardware settings, participants may need to run scans or updates overnight.

In this course, students will learn the fundamentals of programming in Python, and develop applications to demonstrate proficiency.

QuickStart Bootcamp FAQs

We offer cybersecurity, software engineering, web development, data science & analytics, cloud engineering, and AI/ML bootcamps for immersive and flex learning tracks. Select universities offer an accelerated 12-week modality.

The new foundational bootcamp offers 2 hour mentoring sessions twice per week.
