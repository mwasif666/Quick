      Publish Course

      Work done on a course prior to publishing it will only be seen in authoring mode. To make a course, or updates on a course, available to learners, you must publish it.

      Publishing criteria are derived from Settings & Availability pages. If they are displaying incorrectly, please review and modify your selections. requirements. If the red warning icon displays, please review and modify your settings for that item.

        • Course has at least 1 lesson folder
        • Each lesson folder has at least 1 slider
        • Agreement text is provided.

      Release/Update Notes *

      • Show Release notes to Users
      • If your course successfully meets the publishing criteria, you can choose to publish / republish your course.
      • Publish To Store: Directly publishes your course onto the MarketPlace or Branded Resaler Site to offer for others to take or enroll in.
      • Publish To Manager Mode: If you are a manager, this directly publishes your courses to assign and enroll students into directly through your manager mode.
