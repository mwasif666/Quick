Army CA Enrollment Guide For Army Active Duty, National Guard, And Reserve Soldiers

If you are Active Duty Army, Army Reserve or National Guard personnel, you may be eligible for up to $4,000 of Financial Assistance from the Army Credentialing Assistance Program, which is part of the Army Credentialing Opportunities Online (COOL) service. What Is The Army Credentialing Assistance (CA) Program?

The Army Credentialing Assistance (CA) program equips eligible service members with a pathway to career advancement through industry-aligned training and certifications. Eligible individuals may receive up to $4,000 per year toward approved career training.
